# Allowances

Reference extracted from `regulations/regulations.md`, Second Schedule, pages 132-135
of the CAA Service Regulations 2014, revised version 2019. Amounts are monthly
unless noted. "Running basic pay" means the employee's current basic pay.

## EG regular allowances

These apply to EG employees where the employee otherwise meets the normal
eligibility conditions.

| Allowance | EG group | Rate / amount |
|---|---:|---:|
| House Rent | EG-01 and above | 80% of running basic pay |
| Medical Allowance | EG-01 and above | 25% of running basic pay |
| Utility Allowance | EG-01 and above | 25% of running basic pay |
| Entertainment Allowance | EG-01 to EG-02 | Rs. 1,500 |
| Entertainment Allowance | EG-03 to EG-04 | Rs. 2,500 |
| Entertainment Allowance | EG-05 to EG-06 | Rs. 3,500 |
| Entertainment Allowance | EG-07 to EG-08 | Rs. 4,500 |
| Entertainment Allowance | EG-09 to EG-10 | Rs. 5,500 |
| Entertainment Allowance | EG-11 | Rs. 10,500 |
| Car Monetization Allowance | EG-07 and above | As specified by the Authority from time to time |

## EG fuel / conveyance allowance

Fuel / conveyance allowance is calculated as the amount equivalent to the stated
liters of petrol. The rupee amount therefore changes with the applicable petrol
price.

| EG group | Petrol entitlement |
|---|---:|
| EG-01 to EG-02 | 150 liters |
| EG-03 to EG-04 | 175 liters |
| EG-05 to EG-06 | 225 liters |
| EG-07 to EG-10 | Discontinued |

APMs in EG-01 to EG-10 are entitled to fuel allowance equivalent to 150 liters of
petrol if their entitlement under their service pay group is lower.

## EG motor vehicle maintenance allowance

The schedule states motor vehicle maintenance allowance at 10% of minimum basic
pay of the corresponding pay scale. For APMs in EG-01 to EG-10, minimum
entitlement is Rs. 4,000 if the service pay-group entitlement is lower. The
allowance is discontinued for EG-07 to EG-10 employees.

## EG project and instructional allowances

Project allowance is admissible on PCAA infrastructure projects worth Rs. 3
billion and above, only to permanent employees posted on the project.
Instructional allowance is for permanent employees posted as instructors at CATI.

| Allowance | EG group | Maximum amount |

| Project Allowance | EG-01 to EG-04 | Up to Rs. 30,000 |
| Project Allowance | EG-05 to EG-06 | Up to Rs. 45,000 |
| Project Allowance | EG-07 to EG-08 | Up to Rs. 65,000 |
| Project Allowance | EG-09 to EG-10 | Up to Rs. 80,000 |
| Instructional Allowance | EG-01 to EG-04 | Up to Rs. 30,000 |
| Instructional Allowance | EG-05 to EG-06 | Up to Rs. 45,000 |
| Instructional Allowance | EG-07 to EG-08 | Up to Rs. 65,000 |

## Hard area allowance

performing duty in the listed area.
